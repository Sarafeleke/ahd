About Amanawit Design

Discover our story, passion, and commitment to preserving Ethiopian cultural heritage through fashion

Our Story

Amanawit Design was born from a deep love for Ethiopian cultural heritage and a vision to showcase the beauty of traditional Habesha fashion to the world. Founded in Addis Ababa, our brand represents the perfect blend of timeless tradition and contemporary elegance.

We believe that every piece of traditional clothing tells a story - a story of craftsmanship, cultural identity, and artistic expression passed down through generations. Our mission is to preserve these stories while creating new ones for the modern generation.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Ethiopian cultural heritage through authentic, high-quality traditional and modern Habesha fashion that celebrates our identity and connects generations.

Our Vision

To become the leading destination for authentic Ethiopian fashion, showcasing the beauty and elegance of Habesha culture to the world while empowering local artisans and preserving traditional craftsmanship.

Our Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional techniques and cultural significance in every piece we create.

Quality

We use only the finest materials and skilled craftsmanship to ensure lasting beauty and durability.

Heritage

We honor and preserve the rich cultural traditions and techniques passed down through generations.

Innovation

We blend traditional designs with modern aesthetics to create fashion that speaks to today's generation.
